About

TrueWageCalculator.com estimates your true hourly wage by dividing pay by the total time cost of work. For many people, commute time and unpaid work are the hidden pay cut.

What we count

  • Pay: salary or hourly pay (plus optional bonus + benefits you choose to include).
  • Time cost: scheduled hours, unpaid overtime, commute time, unpaid breaks, optional prep time.
  • Weeks worked: 52 minus paid weeks off (PTO + holidays).

How to use the result

  • Compare two jobs: the higher salary isn’t always the higher true wage.
  • Evaluate commute changes: remote or closer work can be a huge “raise.”
  • Spot burnout risk: if unpaid hours are high, your real wage drops fast.

Educational estimate only. Results depend on your inputs and assumptions.
