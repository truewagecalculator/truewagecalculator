Methodology (what “true wage” means here)

  • We start with your pay (salary or hourly), plus optional bonus and benefits.
  • We compute how many weeks you actually work: 52 − paid weeks off.
  • We count time you “pay” with: commute, unpaid overtime, unpaid breaks, and optional prep time.
  • Optional: apply a subjective “stress tax” that reduces effective wage by 0–20%.

Why this matters

Two jobs with the same salary can pay very different “real” wages once you factor in commute time, unpaid overtime, and other unpaid time sinks.

If you’re new to the idea, read our guide on what a true hourly wage really means and how it’s calculated.
